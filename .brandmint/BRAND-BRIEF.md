# Rasa — Brand Brief

> *Taste, balanced.*

**Rasa** (Sanskrit: taste / essence — the Ayurvedic model of a balanced meal) plans a month of meals around your macros and your budget, then orders them for you a tap at a time.

## The one-liner
A month of meals, planned to your macros and budget, ordered with one tap.

## Positioning
- **Category:** AI meal planner + auto-ordering for delivery (health-tech / food-tech).
- **For:** metro IT/tech professionals who already order in daily ("Deadline Deepak"); fitness/macro-trackers next.
- **Core promise:** convenience gets you in the door; **macro adherence + a hard budget cap** are what make Rasa worth paying for.
- **Moat (v2):** neutral cross-platform orchestration — one budget across Swiggy, Zomato, Instamart and tiffin — the seam the platforms structurally will not build.

## Personality & voice
Effortless, balanced, warm, precise, unobtrusive. Archetypes: **Caregiver + Sage.** The competent friend who cooks — never a diet coach yelling macros. "Sorted your lunch, tap to confirm."

## Look
Spice-warm and appetizing, grounded by fresh green and cream — nourishing, not clinical.
- **Primary:** Turmeric Saffron `#E8A33D`
- **Secondary:** Fresh Basil Green `#2F7D5B`
- **Accent:** Ripe Tomato `#E4572E` (confirm moments)
- Real top-down food photography, soft rounded plate/bowl shapes, matte flat surfaces.

## Guardrails
- Health/wellness framing, **never** a convenience-fee framing.
- Nutrition figures are **estimates** (no medical/disease claims — FSSAI-safe).
- **One-tap approve per order**; nothing ordered or spent silently; allergens hard-blocked.
